19. What is Pseudocode?

  • Most programmers spend the majority of their job thinking about code rather than writing it.
  • Programmers use Pseudocode to plan out their code.
  • Pseudocode can be compared to an outline for a paper. Explain the main topics of the essay and plan out the major talking points. Don’t worry about the nitty gritty details (Word Choice, Grammar Conventions, Proper Formatting).
  • Review
  • Flowchart Method (Good for thinking through the flow of a function).
  • Write-Up Method (Good for getting the general idea of a program down).
  • Functionality Planning Method (Good for listing out the functions of a certain program).
